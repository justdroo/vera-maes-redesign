EVENTS & CATERING

Host your next event at Vera Mae’s. We offer a variety of services for small and large parties. Our Ballaird hall makes a perfect location for a rehearsal dinners, award ceremonies, holiday parties or birthday celebrations.

Our cellar downstairs looks out onto the Board Room, a more intimate dining experience ready for your next business meeting.

To discuss planning your next event at Vera Mae’s, or to inquire about catering, please contact our coordinator, Dave Franklin.

dave@veramaes.com

BALLAIRD HALL

Located next door to the bistro, Ballaird hall provides soaring ceilings and an expansive space for your next event. Entire hall is available as well as just the back for smaller events.

  • 10-150 guests
  • Large open space
  • Good for rehersal dinners, reunions, large birthdays and other events

BOARD ROOM

A private space underneath the restaurant, the Board Room has a projector screen for business meetings and can fit up to thirty two guests.

  • 10-32 guests
  • Quiet, intimate room
  • Good for presentations, business meeting, smaller parties

CATERING

Bring Vera Mae's to your home or office. Full bar available along with appetizers, buffet and dining options available.

  • 10-150+ guests
  • Hand served appetizers, three-course dinner available
  • Perfect for weddings, parties in you home or office holidays
